WHO WE ARE

No limit is a private studio. We offer personal training 1 on 1, personalized routines. We also have a partner program and group of 3. Our studio is a comfortable environment where you can train the way you want and also do your photoshoots

Discover our space

Unlike other personal training studios, which conceptually entails an exercise regimen that is tailored for each individual, No Limit Personal Training Studio will offer the client a true "VIP" service where the client will see the real value and benefits. In addition to an exclusive and personalized exercise plan, the client will also be provided a nutrition plan designed for each client based on their tastes, amenities and lifestyle. Each client will have a calendar with their records and scheduled progress evaluations, which will keep each client motivated. The client will have short and long-term goals that we will establish together, and each goal achieved will be rewarded with a small incentive, be it a free training session, a discount for purchases at No-Limit Personal Training Studio, supplements, or a gift card. Something simple that will recognize and reward your achievements and provide you an incentive to continue to achieve your overall personal goals. In addition, No-Limit Personal Training Studio will offer presentations, clinics, etc. related to fitness and healthy lifestyles on a regular basis, so that clients can be more informed about how to maintain and/or improve an overall healthy lifestyle.
